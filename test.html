School Exhibition Wins State Award!

Published on October 15, 2025

By The School Times Reporter

The annual school science exhibition, held at City Modern School, was awarded the top honors at the State Science Festival this week. Hundreds of students from our city participated, showcasing innovations in robotics, environment, and healthcare.

Highlights

  • Over 25 innovative projects displayed
  • Guest lecture by Dr. Maya Verma
  • Robot-building competition was a crowd favorite

Awarded Projects

  1. Solar-Powered Water Filter
  2. Smart Reminder Robot
  3. Low-cost Wheelchair Design

"We are extremely proud of our students' achievements. Their hard work and creativity are truly inspiring," said Principal Arjun Kumar.
